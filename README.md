# DeFi University Glossary

A comprehensive glossary of terms, definitions, and concepts extracted from the DeFi University course materials.

## Overview

This glossary contains over 100 key terms and definitions covering all aspects of decentralized finance (DeFi), organized alphabetically for easy reference. Each term includes:

- Clear, concise definitions
- Source course attribution
- Mathematical formulas (where applicable)
- Cross-references to related terms
- Examples and context

## Source Courses

The glossary is compiled from the following DeFi University courses:

- **Beginner DeFi Concepts** - Foundational blockchain and DeFi concepts
- **Liquidity Provision** - AMM mechanics, impermanent loss, and LP strategies
- **Money Markets** - Lending, borrowing, and protocol architectures
- **Perpetual Futures Trading** - Derivatives, funding rates, and trading mechanics
- **Investor Mindset** - Trading psychology and cognitive biases

## Usage

### Browsing the Glossary

The glossary is organized alphabetically with a table of contents for quick navigation. Each term includes:

- **Definition**: Clear explanation of the concept
- **Source**: Which course(s) the term appears in
- **Related Terms**: Cross-references to related concepts
- **Formulas**: Mathematical representations (where applicable)

### Finding Terms

1. Use the table of contents to jump to a letter section
2. Use your browser's search function (Ctrl+F / Cmd+F) to find specific terms
3. Follow cross-references to explore related concepts

### Mathematical Formulas

Key formulas are included in LaTeX format. These include:

- Constant Product Formula (x · y = k)
- Health Factor calculations
- Funding Rate formulas
- Margin requirements
- Price impact calculations
